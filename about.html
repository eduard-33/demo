The Story of Giovanni Menswear

Giovanni Menswear was founded in 2018 by Giovanni Russo, a second-generation tailor raised in Milan’s historic fashion district. Inspired by his father’s atelier and a lifelong appreciation for refined menswear, Giovanni set out to create a brand that blended traditional tailoring with modern simplicity.

What began as a local tailoring studio has since grown into a recognized name for men’s fashion — offering everything from sharp blazers and smart trousers to minimalist streetwear. Every piece we create reflects a balance between classic craftsmanship and contemporary design.

At Giovanni Menswear, quality is personal. We handpick fabrics from trusted European mills and focus on clean lines, comfort, and detail. Whether you’re dressing for a meeting, a night out, or everyday style, we believe the right fit can change how you feel.

Giovanni’s mission is simple: make men feel confident in clothes that speak quietly, but powerfully.
